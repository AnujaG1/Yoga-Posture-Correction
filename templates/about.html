Discover the Power of Yoga

Halasana

Halasana is a powerful pose that stretches the spine and shoulders, calms the nervous system, and improves flexibility. It helps stimulate the thyroid gland, improves digestion, and relieves stress and fatigue.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Vrikshasana improves balance and stability in the legs, and helps strengthen the thighs, calves, and spine. It also enhances your concentration and mindfulness while promoting a sense of grounding.

T-Pose (Standing Balance Pose)

The T-Pose is a simple yet powerful standing posture that improves posture, strengthens the arms and back, and brings awareness to body alignment.

Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Savasana is a restorative pose usually practiced at the end of a yoga session. It promotes deep relaxation and mental stillness.

Peacock Pose (Mayurasana)

Mayurasana is an advanced arm balance pose that symbolizes strength and grace. It strengthens the wrists, forearms, and core muscles.

Camel Pose (Ustrasana)

Ustrasana is a deep backbend that stretches the front body and improves spinal flexibility while stimulating the respiratory system.
